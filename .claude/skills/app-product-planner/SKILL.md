name: app-product-planner
description: Plan a new app or feature before implementation. Scope, risks, definition of done. Use via /project:plan.
---

Before implementing anything that takes over 30 minutes.

Plan format:
Goal: [one sentence]
Scope: [in / explicitly out]
Steps: [ordered]
Definition of done: [evidence that proves completion]
Risks: [top 2-3]
Do not build yet: [scope boundary]

Ask for confirmation before implementing.
